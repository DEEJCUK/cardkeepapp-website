üöÄ Getting Started

Welcome to CardKeepApp! This beautiful app helps you preserve your precious greeting card memories forever. Here's how to get started:

First Launch

When you first open CardKeepApp, you'll see a clean, welcoming interface designed specifically for iPhone / iPad. The app uses a warm, cosy aesthetic that makes organising your memories a delightful experience.

Interface Overview

Collection View

Your main screen shows all your saved cards in a beautiful grid layout. Each card displays a preview of the front photo.

Add Button

The prominent "+" button lets you quickly add new cards to your collection.

Search & Filter

Powerful search tools help you find any card instantly using multiple criteria.

üì∏ Adding Cards to Your Collection

CardKeepApp makes it easy to preserve every detail of your greeting cards with multi-photo storage.

Enhanced Photo Capture System

CardKeepApp features an improved photo type selection system that makes it clear and intuitive to organise your card photos. For each card, you can capture up to 5 photos with our enhanced interface:

  • Front: The outside cover design
  • Inside: The heartfelt message inside

‚ú® Recent Improvements: We've completely redesigned the photo type selection interface to be clearer and more user-friendly. The app now provides better visual cues when choosing which part of your card you're photographing, eliminating previous labeling confusion.

üíæ Backup & Export System

CardKeepApp includes a comprehensive backup system to protect your precious memories and allow you to transfer your collection between devices.

JSON Backup Export

Export your entire card collection to a lightweight JSON file that contains all your card information and photo references:

Complete Card Data

All card information including sender, occasion, dates, tags, and personal notes

Photo References

Links to your photos in the Photos app (photos remain in your Photos library)

Ultra-Lightweight

Export files are less than 1KB per card)

Easy Sharing

Share via email, AirDrop, or save to Files app

Import & Restore

Restore your collection from backup files:

  • Duplicate Detection: Automatically identifies and prevents duplicate cards during import
  • Data Validation: Ensures backup files are valid before importing
  • Photo Reference Recovery: Attempts to reconnect with photos in your Photos app
  • Selective Import: Choose which cards to import from backup files

iCloud Integration

üí° iCloud Benefits: When iCloud is available, CardKeepApp can automatically detect iCloud status and provide helpful guidance for backing up your collection across devices.

Backup Reminders

CardKeepApp includes smart backup reminders to help protect your memories:

  • Monthly Prompts: Gentle reminders to backup your collection
  • Card Count Tracking: Shows how many cards you have to backup
  • Last Backup Date: Tracks when you last created a backup
‚ö†Ô∏è Important Backup Notes:
  • Backup files contain photo references, not the actual photos
  • Photos must remain in your Photos app for successful restore
  • When moving to a new device, transfer photos first, then import backup
  • Backup files work best when photos are synced via iCloud Photos

üîç Advanced Search Features

CardKeepApp's smart search helps you find any card instantly using powerful search capabilities.

Search by Single Criteria

Sender Search: Find all cards from "Mum", "Sarah", or "Grandma"
Occasion Search: Find all "Birthday", "Wedding", "Thank You", or "Get Well" cards
Date Search: Find cards from specific years, months, or date ranges
Tag Search: Find cards with specific custom tags like "Family", "Work", "Special"

Multi-Criteria Search

CardKeepApp's search is powerful enough to combine multiple criteria:

Date + Sender: "All birthday cards from Mum in 2023"
Tags + Occasion: "Thank you cards tagged as 'Work'"
Multiple Tags: Cards with both "Family" AND "Special" tags
Date Range + Tags: "Cards from 2022-2023 with 'Anniversary' tag"

Search Tips

  • Search is case-insensitive, so "mum" finds "Mum"
  • Partial matches work - "birth" finds "Birthday"
  • Date search supports year, month/year, or full dates
  • Multiple tags can be searched simultaneously
  • Search includes both visible text and your custom notes

üîí Data & Privacy

Your precious memories deserve the highest level of privacy and security.

Local Storage

All your card data is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's Core Data framework. This means:

  • No data is sent to CardKeepApp servers
  • No third parties can access your cards
  • Your memories stay completely private
  • Works offline - no internet connection required

Advanced Photos App Integration

CardKeepApp uses a sophisticated photo reference system with the latest iOS technologies (iOS 16.0+) for optimal performance and reliability:

  • PHAsset Integration: Uses modern PHAsset identifiers for robust photo references
  • CardKeepApp Album: Photos are saved to a dedicated "CardKeepApp" album in your Photos app
  • Smart Reference System: Stores lightweight photo references instead of binary copies
  • Enhanced Performance: Improved photo loading with smart caching and thumbnail refresh
  • Automatic iCloud Sync: If iCloud Photos is enabled, your card photos sync automatically
  • Storage Optimisation: Reduces app storage by 90%+ compared to binary storage
  • Comprehensive Error Handling: Professional-grade error management and permissions handling
  • Backward Compatibility: Seamless support for existing cards during system improvements
  • Important: Deleting photos from the Photos app will break the link in CardKeepApp
üîß Production-Ready Architecture: CardKeepApp uses professional-grade Core Data integration, smart caching systems, and the latest iOS photo management APIs to ensure reliability, performance, and future compatibility.

Permissions

CardKeepApp only requests essential permissions:

  • Camera: To photograph your greeting cards
  • Photo Library: To save card photos to your device

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Limitations

Understanding these limitations helps you use CardKeepApp most effectively.

‚ö†Ô∏è No Editing After Saving: Once you save a card, you cannot edit the text information (sender, occasion, date, tags, notes). You can view and enjoy your cards, but changes require deleting and re-adding the card.
‚ö†Ô∏è iOS Only: Currently CardKeepApp is available for iPhone and iPad. Android version is planned for the future.
‚ö†Ô∏è Portrait Orientation Only: On iPhone, the app is designed exclusively for portrait mode and will not rotate to landscape orientation. iPad supports both portrait and landscape orientations.
‚ö†Ô∏è No Automatic Cloud Sync: Cards don't automatically sync between devices, but you can use the backup/export system to transfer your collection manually.
‚ö†Ô∏è Data Deletion Warning: If you delete the CardKeepApp app, all your saved card data will be lost forever unless you have exported the data using our export function. Your photos remain safely stored in your Photos app and assigned to the "CardKeepApp" album.
‚ö†Ô∏è Photo Deletion Warning: Since CardKeepApp references photos stored in your Photos app, deleting photos from the "CardKeepApp" album in your Photos app will break the links in CardKeepApp. There's no way to re-add photos to existing cards.
‚ö†Ô∏è Share vs Export: You can share your card data and photos, but this shares the main information and photos, not the exact display format from the app.

Planning Your Cards

Since you cannot edit cards after saving, it's helpful to:

  • Double-check all information before saving
  • Think about your tagging system in advance
  • Take all the photos you want before completing the card entry
  • Write notes that will be meaningful to you later

‚ú® Tips & Tricks

Make the most of CardKeepApp with these helpful tips.

Photography Tips

  • Good Lighting: Take photos in bright, even lighting for best results
  • Proper Framing: Frame the card properly to fill most of the screen - this is crucial as you cannot crop or edit photos after saving. The camera interface allows you to take photos in both portrait and landscape orientations to best match your card's format
  • Steady Shots: Use both hands and ensure the card is positioned straight
  • Multiple Angles: Capture front, inside, back, and any special details
  • Readable Text: Ensure handwritten messages are clearly visible and in focus

Organisation Tips

  • Consistent Naming: Use consistent formats for senders (e.g., "Mum" not "Mother" sometimes)
  • Descriptive Tags: Use specific tags that will help you find cards later
  • Date Accuracy: Enter the date you received the card, and the date of the occasion if different
  • Personal Notes: Include context about why the card is special to you

Search Tips

  • Start Broad: Begin with general terms, then narrow down
  • Try Variations: Search "birth" if "birthday" doesn't find everything
  • Use Multiple Terms: Combine sender names with occasions or dates
  • Check Tags: Remember to search your custom tags too
