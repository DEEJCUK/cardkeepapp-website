Why People Use CardKeepApp

Some cards are hard to throw away. CardKeepApp gives you a simple way to keep the messages, photos, and details that still matter without needing to store every paper card forever.
Read the guide to see how the app works in more detail.

  • Built for iPhone and iPad with a simple card-first layout
  • Keep sender details, occasions, notes, tags, and photos together
  • Works well for birthday, thank you, congratulations, and everyday cards
  • Private by design with data stored on your device
  • Available on the App Store in supported regions
  • Fully localised interface available in 8+ languages
  • Smart date handling that adapts to your region's format
  • Flexible backups to help you move or restore your collection
  • Easy photo capture and library import for older cards
  • Made for keeping meaningful cards accessible, not tucked away
